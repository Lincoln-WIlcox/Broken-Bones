
Broken Bones


Main Concept - You work for a museum with low standards, and your job is to glue together dinosaur bones in whatever recognizable way possible.

Features:

Bones come on table somehow

Sticker like-bone placement

Name dinosaurs

you make exhibits

3 dinos are an exhibit

Timer limits how long you have before you fail
	-Timer doesn't start until you drag first piece
	-Progress bar type timer
	-When timer runs out, you lose your dinosaur bones and get no points

Pedastals in background with your previous creations
